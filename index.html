Research

Research Areas

01

Ergonomics

We focus on optimizing interactions between people and their environment to improve efficiency, safety, and well-being.

02

Biomechanics

The application of biological and medical principles to solve health-related problems, including human biology, disease mechanisms, and development of medical devices and diagnostics.

03

Occupational Health & Safety

We focus on protecting employee well-being in the workplace through practices, regulations, and strategies to prevent injuries, illnesses, and fatalities.

04

Human-Centered Product Design & Evaluation

We create user-friendly products and services by prioritizing user needs, behaviors, and preferences throughout the design and development process.
